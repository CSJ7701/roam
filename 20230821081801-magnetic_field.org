#+title: Magnetic Field
#+filetags: :Antennas:

Magnetic Field - an invisible force field created by either a magnet or a current flow.

$$B = \frac{\mu I}{2\pi d}(T)$$ and $$B = \mu H$$

Where $\mu$ = Magnetic Permeability $(H/m)$

$I$ = current
$d$ = distance
$\mu = \mu_{r}\mu_{o}$
$\mu_r = 1$ (air)
$\mu_0 = 4\pi*10^{-7} (H/m)$
$H =$ magnetic field intensity (Amps/Meter)
$B =$ Magnetic Flux Density (Tesla or Webers/meter squared)

*Note:*
A time varying charge -> DC current -> constant Magnetic Field
An accelerating Charge -> AC Current -> time varying Magnetic Field
