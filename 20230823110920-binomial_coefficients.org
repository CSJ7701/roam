#+title: Binomial Coefficients
#+filetags: :ProbTheory:

$(x+y)^{2} = x^2 + 2xy +y^2$
$(x+y)^3 = x^3 + 3x^2 y + 3xy^2 +y^3$

The coefficients are chosen with Pascal's triangle, but we can also use combinations.
The first term would be $\binom{3}{3}$, second would be $\binom{3}{2}$, etc.
