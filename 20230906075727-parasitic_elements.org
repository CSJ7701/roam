#+title: Parasitic Elements
#+filetags: :Antennas:

Since a "Pure" elements don't exist, all wires have some amount of inductance/capacitance.

This is known as parasitic capacitance or inductance. 

Leads become parasitic, in general, once their length is greater than 0.01*Wavelength
