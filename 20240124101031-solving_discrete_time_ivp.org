#+title: Solving Discrete Time IVP
#+filetags: :DSP:

Method of undetermined coefficients

Ex:
Find the response y(a) for n=0 of the system
$y(n)-3y(n-1)-4y(n-2)=x(n)+2x(n-1)$ let $x(n)=4^{n}u(n)$

Steps:
1. find $y_{n}$
2. determine $y_{p} from table
3. plug $y_{p}$ into system and solve for coeff
4. find ICs recursively
5. plug into y to find $y_{n}$ coeff


-- *1* --

Let $y_{n}(n)=\gamma^{n}$
$\gamma=e^{\lambda}$
$\gamma^{n}-3\gamma^{n-1}-4\gamme^{n-2}=0$
$\gamma^{n-2}(\gamma^{2}-3\gamma-4)=0$
$\gamma_{1}=4;\\\gamma_{2}=-1$
Therefore $y_{n}(n)=C_{1}(-1)^n +C_{2}(4)^n$

-- *2* --

Our input is $x(n)=4^{n}u(n)$
From the table, we see A=1, M=4
$\therefore y_{p}=kn(4)^{n}$

-- *3* --

$kn(4)^{n}u(n)-3k(n-1)(4)^{n-1}u(n-1)-4k(n-2)4^{n-2}u(n-2)=4^{n}u(n)+2(4)^{n-1}u(n-1)$
Factor $4^n$ from both sides and cancel.
Let $n\ge2$
$kn-\frac{3}{4}k-\frac{1}{4}kn+\frac{1}{2}k=\frac{3}{2}$
$k=\frac{6}{5}
$y_{p}(n)=\frac{6}{5}n(4)^{n}$
We know $y=y_{n}+y_{p}$
$\therefore y=c_{1}(-1)^{n}+c_{2}(4)^{n}+\frac{6}{5}n(4)^{n}$

-- *4* --
Let n=0
$y(0)-3y(-1)-4y(-2)=x(0)+2x(-1)$
$y(0)=x(0)=4^{0}u(0)=1$
Let n=1
$y(1)-3y(0)-4y(-1)=x(1)+2x(0)$
