#+title: Cartesian Product of Sets
#+filetags: :Discrete:

$A\times B=\{(a,b)|a\in A,b\in B\}$

E.g.

$A=\{1,2\}$
$B=\{3,4\}$
$A\times B=\{(1,3),(1,4),(2,3),(2,4)\}$
