#+title: Butterworth Filter
#+filetags: :Linear:

Butterworth Filter - "maximally flat" magnitude response

Butterworth Response: $|H(j\omega)|^{2} = \frac{1}{1+(\frac{\omega}{\omega_{0}})^{2n}}$

Where $\omega_0$ is half power or -3 dB frequency
*Note* - $|H(j\omega)|^{2} = H(j\omega) * H^{*}(j\omega)$ - H times H conjugate
