#+title: General Operations
#+filetags: :DSP:

Let $x_1(n)={3,2,1,0,1,2,3}$
Let $x_2(n)={0,0,0,1,1,1,1}$

$x_1(n)+x_2(n)$ - element-wise
$x_1(n)*x_2(n)$ - element-wise

* Time Shifting
$x(n-n_0)$ - delay
$x(n+n_0)$ - advance

* Time Reversal (Folding)
$x(-n)$ - flips over $n=0$

/note/:
$x(n)\rightarrow x(n-n_0)\rightarrow x(-n-n_0)$
- Shift then fold

$x(n)\rightarrow x(-n)\rightarrow x(-n+x_0)$
- Fold then shift
