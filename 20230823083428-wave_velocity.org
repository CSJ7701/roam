#+title: Wave Velocity
#+filetags: :Antennas:

$\mu_{p} = \lambda f$

$\mu_{p} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\varepsilon\mu}}$
Where:
$\varepsilon = \varepsilon_{r}\varepsilon_{0}$
$\mu = \mu_{r}\mu_{0}$

Wave velocity /always/ decreases when passing through non-vacuum media - EVEN AIR.
