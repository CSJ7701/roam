The wave varies in time t, and distance x.
$\phi$ = phase (radians or degrees)
$\lambda$ = wavelength (meters)
A = Amplitude
T = period
$f = \frac{1}{T}$ = frequency (Hz)
$\omega = 2f\pi$ = frequency in radians/sec
