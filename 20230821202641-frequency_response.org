#+title: Frequency Response
#+filetags: :SST:Linear:

Frequency Response: a function that expresses /exactly/ how a filter responds to sinusoidal inputs at different frequencies.
The magnitude of frequency response is known as [[id:3a9a668a-5f74-4ecc-a02c-c099361bc890][Magnitude Response]].
