#+title: Interprocess Communication
#+filetags: :OS:

Concurrent Processes are independent, but cooperate.

This allows them to share information, speeds up operation (since they aren't running as one large program).

There are two ways to implement this:
- [[id:5b9fc7a4-a87a-47ef-b903-43fb5350801e][Shared Memory]]
- [[id:911bf314-6501-4e8d-a802-69d9ec5ad182][Message Passing]]
