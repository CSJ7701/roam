#+filetags: :ProbTheory:

Rule of Total Probability
If the events $B_1, B_2, B_3, ... B_k$ constitutes a partition of the sample space $S+P(Bi)\ne 0$ for $i=1...k$
then for any event A in S,
$P(A)=\sum_{i=1}^{k}P(B_i)P(A|B_i)$
