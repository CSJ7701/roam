#+title: Standing Wave Ratio
#+filetags: :Antennas:

$S = \frac{|V_{\text{max}}|}{|V_{\text{min}}} = \frac{1+|\Gamma|}{1-|\Gamma|}$

We also know that $z_{L} = \frac{Z_{L}}{Z_0}$

From this:
$\Gamma=\frac{z_{L}-1}{z_{L}+1}$

$|\Gamma|=\frac{S-1}{S+1}$
