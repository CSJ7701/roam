#+title: Bels 
#+filetags: :Linear:

Bel $\stackrel{\triangle}{=} log_{10}(\frac{P_{Out}}{P_{In}})$
dB = DeciBel = $$\stackrel{\triangle}{=} 10 log_{10}(\frac{P_{out}}{P_{in}} = 10 log_{10}(\frac{V_{out}^{2} / R}{V_{in}^{2} / R}) = 20 log_{10}(\frac{V_{out}}{V_{in}})$$

$$\therefore dBm = 10log_{10}(\frac{P_{out}}{1 mW})$$
$$\therefore dBv = 20log_{10}(\frac{V_{out}}{1V})$$
