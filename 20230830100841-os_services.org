#+title: OS Services
#+filetags: :OS:


Services are the underlying proceses that run in order to support other applications and functions, like a GUI, etc.
Typically, services operate /below/ [[id:d9152677-08e7-46aa-9797-b4772ef1a32d][Interfaces]] like a GUI.

Such interfaces make [[id:88a26516-aef3-4b21-b0c0-6628e8c2a5d7][System Calls]] down to services in order to accomplish tasks.
