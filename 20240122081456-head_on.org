#+title: Head On
#+filetags: :MWO:

2 PDV on a "reciprocal or nearly reciprocal" course.
Mast Head Lights will appear in or near to a line (or corresponding aspect by day)
When in doubt, assume head on.

/Inland only/: When proceeding with a following current
- Only valid on western rivers
- Has right of way over the vessel proceeding upriver.
- This is because the vessel travelling upriver has more water passing over their rudder; they have greater steerageway.

Must alter course to starboard and pass port to port.
