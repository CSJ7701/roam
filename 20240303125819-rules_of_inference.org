#+title: Rules of Inference
#+filetags: :Discrete:

* Modus Ponens

$\begin{array}{ }
&p & p\implies q \cline{2-2} q
\end{array}$

* Modus Tollens

$\begin{array}{ }
&p\implies q &\lnot q \cline{2-2} \lnot p\end{array}$

* Disjunctive Syllogism
$\begin{array}{ }
& p \lor\ q
& \lnot p
\cline{2-2}
q\end{array}$


* Chain Rule
$\begin{array}
&p\implies q
&q\implies r
\cline{2-2}
p\implies r
\end{array}$

* Resolution
$\begin{array}
&p\lor r
&q\lor(\lnot r)
\cline{2-2}
p\lor q
\end{array}$
